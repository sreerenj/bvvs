About Us

Vaidyakalanidhi Sri. N. Bhaskaran Nair, a visionary in the field of ayurveda, who has unwavering commitment and profound healing potential established gus dedication in the form of a small village clinic in the year 1941. Later this modest form grows into a well established production unit and a hospital by his legacy.

Now we are manufacturing more than 250 classical medicinal preparations without losing the authenticity. Apart from this, we have stepped ahead of developing new formulations taking references from authoritative scriptures of ayurveda.
